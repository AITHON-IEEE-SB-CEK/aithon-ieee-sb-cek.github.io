AITHON 2.0

Crafting future Signals with AI

Get ready for AITHON 2.0, the ultimate 24-hour AI Signal Processing hackathon brought to you by IEEE Computer Society and Signal Processing Society College of Engineering Karunagappally! We challenge you to tackle innovative and sustainable solutions for cutting-edge AI and Signal Processing hurdles. AITHON 2.0 is more than just a 2 day event—it's a complete experience. Here you can dive into brainstorming sessions, panel discussions, and hands-on expert sessions, all in one place. Dont miss this chance to network with industry leaders, AI pioneers, and fellow enthusiasts in an exciting and high-energy environment. No matter if you’re noob or pro, we have something for everyone. You could win cool goodies and exciting prizes, but more importantly, you'll leave with new skills and perspectives. No matter if you've never tried solving problems before, you'll step out transformed and inspired. Join us at AITHON 2.0 and be part of something truly transformative.

Guidelines for the Hackathon





  • Be at the Registration Desk by 9 AM sharp.

  • Bring a valid ID to enter.

  • Show a soft copy of your IEEE membership card at registration, For IEEE CS/SPS members, provide your corresponding membership card soft copy.

  • Each team must consist of 2 to 4 members.

  • Every team must have a unique team name.

  • There should be atleast one laptop for each team.

  • A cloakroom will be provided inside the venue. However, the organizers won't be held responsible for any lost wallets, ornaments, etc.

  • Vehicles are not permitted inside the campus.

  • Mentoring sessions will take place on August 31st.

  • The hackathon kicks off at 3:00 PM on August 31st.

  • The Problem Statement will be revealed before the hackathon begins..

  • Teams must stop coding when the time is up but can debug and make minor fixes. Adding new features or making significant changes after that is not permitted.

  • Fresh-up rooms will be available on the second day.

  • No refunds will be processed after payment.

  • Don’t leave the premises without valid consent from event coordinators.
