AITHON

An AI Based Hackathon.

IEEE Computer Society, Student Branch College of Engineering Karunagappally presents AITHON- Let AI Handle it .it is a 24 hour AI Hackathon .The event will be happening on 30th August to 2nd Octobber, within the 24 hours the participants have to provide a feasible and sustainable solution for the problem statement given related to Artificial Intelligence prior session will be organize to create basic foundations of AI to the students out there, it would be a great opportunity to put in the skills to great astounding and revolutionary solutions .
