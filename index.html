AITHON

An AI Based Hackathon.

IEEE Computer Society Student Branch College of Engineering Karunagappally presents AITHON - A National Level 24-hour AI Hackathon.The event will be happening on September 30 October 1st and October 2nd 2022, within the 24 hours the participants have to provide a feasible and sustainable solution for the problem statement given related to Artificial Intelligence prior session will be organised to create basic foundations of AI for the students out there, it would be a great opportunity to put in the skills to great astounding and revolutionary solutions. Winners stand a chance to bag exciting prizes and all participants get to walk away with cool goodies.
